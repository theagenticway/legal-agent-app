AGREEMENT OF SERVICE

This agreement is made on June 1, 2024, between Innovate Corp ("The Client") and Legal Solutions LLC ("The Provider").

1. SCOPE OF WORK: The Provider will offer legal consultation services regarding intellectual property law. The initial term of this agreement shall be for 12 months.

2. PAYMENT: The Client agrees to pay a monthly retainer of $5,000 USD.

3. CONFIDENTIALITY: Both parties agree to keep all shared information confidential. This clause survives the termination of this agreement. The governing law for this agreement shall be that of the State of California.

4. TERMINATION: This agreement can be terminated by either party with a 30-day written notice.
